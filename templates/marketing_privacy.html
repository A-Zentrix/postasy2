Privacy Policy

Effective: {{ (now() if now else '2025') }}

1. What we collect

Account details (name, email), content you upload (images, logos, posters), and usage data (feature interactions, device info). Payment is processed by Stripe—Postasy never stores full card details.

2. How we use data

To provide the service, improve templates and AI features, prevent abuse, and support billing. We may send product emails; you can unsubscribe anytime.

3. Your control
  • Access, export, or delete your data by contacting support.
  • Delete generated posters and uploads from your dashboard.
  • Disable analytics cookies in your browser settings.
4. Sharing

We share data with trusted processors (hosting, analytics, payments) under strict agreements. We never sell personal data.

5. Security

We apply encryption in transit, access controls, and continuous monitoring. No method is perfect—report issues to us immediately.

6. Children

Postasy is not intended for children under 13. If we learn we collected such data, we will delete it.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be announced in‑app or via email.
